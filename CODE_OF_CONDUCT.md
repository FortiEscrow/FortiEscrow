# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing opinions
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being professional in all communications

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments or attacks
- Disruptive behavior
- Any form of abuse

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent removal

## Reporting

Report violations to: conduct@example.com

We commit to promptly and fairly addressing concerns.

---

**All community members agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.**
